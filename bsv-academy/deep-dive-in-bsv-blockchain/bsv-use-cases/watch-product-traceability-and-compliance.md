### Trace

Trace, a project by **Gate2Chain** in Spain, focuses on **Digital Product Passports (DPPs)**, which are becoming a **regulatory requirement** in the EU.

* Ensures comprehensive **product traceability** from sourcing to resale, enhancing **transparency** in supply chains.
* Applications include **regulatory compliance** for various sectors, such as clothing, food, and luxury goods.
* Empowers consumers by proving **provenance** and usage history, which can affect **resale value**, particularly for luxury items.
* Currently running **pilots** in industries like art, fashion, and food, showcasing the versatility of blockchain in compliance and consumer trust.
